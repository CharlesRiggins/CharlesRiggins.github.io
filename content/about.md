Hi, I'm Charles Chen — an AI engineer and machine learning practitioner working on
large language models.

I'm currently an MLE at **Tencent**, working on algorithms and infrastructure for KV-cache in LLMs.
I'm also interested in understanding how post-training methods (such as SFT, RL, and on-policy distillation) shape model behaviors.

Before Tencent, I was a founding engineer at **DeepLang AI**, where I
worked across the full LLM stack: pretraining and post-training, inference
acceleration, and distributed serving.

I earned my B.S. in Computer Science from **Peking University** in 2022 and spent a year as a visiting student at **UC Berkeley**.

I was born in China and my Chinese name is 陈立千 (Liqian Chen).
